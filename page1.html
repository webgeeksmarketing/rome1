OUR STORY

Since our original founding in 2000, Rome has transformed from a 1-office freight brokerage, to a 10+ office, multifaceted, global 3PL provider. We began by planting our roots in freight brokerage, but have since expanded organically into numerous other dimensions of the freight world, including contract freight, fully outsourced 3PL solutions, customs brokerage, project management, and more. Year after year, Rome continues to grow by reinforcing and maintaining our values of trust and excellent customer service. We have always stressed the importance of understanding each customers unique requirements and dilemmas, and have been able to become supported by continuous improvement of tailored solutions. In our earlier days, Rome found the key to success within the logistics world began with personalized representatives, customer specific solutions, and enforcing accountability. In recent years, we have come to appreciate small teams and unique offices environments, staying true to our roots while growing autonomously. Leveraging technology by building in house, and connecting all systems has been a crucial element to allowing numerous office spaces to flourish within Romes larger corporate environment. A passion for getting it done right, and building trust is at the heart of it for Rome and our employees, and after all these years, as we continue to grow and develop, we can confidently attest that this is just the beginning of something much larger.

IN THE FUTURE

No company can create everything they want overnight, after all, Rome wasn't built in a day. The Rome Logistics Group, however, is as forward focused as it gets. It isn't as easy as just hiring the right employees, strengthening niche specialties, developing new technologies or introducing new services, although these things are paramount to Rome's growth plan. For us, the key to growth is focusing on the customer experience. Although this may seem simple enough, for us we take pride in staying fixated on feedback from customers, keeping a close eye on trends and keeping communication open between all parties.
Success in the logistics/3PL marketplace is driven by maintaining and building relationships, developing new connections and always increasing an intelligent network of partnerships and affiliations. As we continue to help our clients succeed and grow, we will continue to expand our offices into as many key locations throughout North America to better develop these relationships and connections. Our small team approach is one which is quite unique in the industry and keeps every client dealing with a small team of a few people who know the account intimately and can give accurate information as it is requested.
