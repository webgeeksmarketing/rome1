WHY ROME LOGISTICS GROUP?

It’s simple really; everyone has the desire to be apart of an organization that offers opportunities for growth, colleagues that are passionate about their work, and a sense of fulfillment when you leave every day. Here at Rome, we have developed a culture and business model that truly incorporates these aspects which, regardless of the position you are in, you will experience on a daily basis. We understand that business as usual is not always good enough, and consistently look for ways to grow and improve. We also understand that in order for a business to grow and improve, we must focus on allowing it to come from the bottom-up, as much as possible! Our upper-level management acknowledges that they aren’t always the ones on the front-lines, and feedback from those who are, both positive and constructive, is key to initiating growth!

If you are not yet convinced of how awesome we are here at Rome, we will let our Great Place to Work® certifications do the talking for us. Not only were we certified as a Great Place to Work® back in July of 2019, but we were also certified on the 2019 list of Best Workplaces ™ for Millennials and the 2020 list of Best Workplaces in Canada.

So, what are you waiting for? Your opportunity to become a Roman is right around the corner!
