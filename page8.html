MODES OF TRANSPORTATION

Equipped to handle your freight requirements at any scale, from one pallet shipping to oversize-with-permits.

Truckload 

When it comes to truckload, businesses stretching across North America and abroad trust Rome to get their freight from point A, to point B. Every day, Rome provides countless full truckload solutions across North America on every lane imaginable, no matter the deadline or the destination.

From one-time pick ups, to dedicated fleets and lanes, Rome has you covered. Equipped with the knowledge, the contacts, and the resources, Rome provides a solution for all of your freight and supply chain needs including, but certainly not limited to; dry-van, heated service, refrigerated service, domestic, cross-border, interstate, multi-picks, multi-drops, hazardous materials, and so much more.

By choosing Rome, you are choosing flexible, efficient, and effective supply chain solutions. Truckload service with Rome means not being limited in your options when it comes to transit time and capacity. Most importantly it means having an expert in your corner to get you through whatever your truckload needs are.  

Less-Than-Truckload

Here at Rome Transportation we handle any inquiry you have, from full loads to less than truck load, all the way to multi-drop options throughout North America. Our cultivated network of less-than-truckload and hundred-weight carriers allows us to confidently extends our reach to service any point in Canada, the United States, and Mexico. We also offer rail, air and ocean solutions for your long hauls and remote locations. Our strategic partnership of carriers allows Rome Transportation to provide premier LTL services that incorporate expediting, timely tracing, customized pick-up and delivery schedules and of course a hands-free service provided by your own personal representative from Rome Transport!

Outside of our strategic network of carriers, Rome Transport has internal departments that allow Rome to handle our customers’ freight, as if it were our own. Our internal Overages, Shortages and Damages department is expertly trained to handle and claim inquiries should they come up, and our additional insurance capabilities allow our customers’ peace of mind when shipping high-value freight. Our dedication to risk mitigation does not stop here however, as our carrier compliance department ensures that from the very beginning, Rome’s carrier network is made up of the best and most reliable carriers that will fulfill our customer service standards. We also offer customs clearance both to and from the USA, and a specialized LTL department to handle any discrepancies or specialized quoting.. Rome Transport can provide a hands-free service from pick-up to delivery, from selecting the carrier, providing additional insurance, customs clearance all the way to handling claims should issues arise after delivery. Rome Transport can do it all! 

Intermodal 

When shipping long haul, it is always important to consider your options. Your Rome representative can help you navigate the possibilities of moving your freight over North America’s extensive rail network. Rome has access to all major ramps and ports, offering drayage capacity for door to door service with 53’ intermodal containers, along with 40’ and 20’ ocean containers. Cross border, multi-modal, container drop are all no problem! 

Flatbed 

Rome Transport has access to many different vehicle types. We provide specialty services for heavy freight, oversized freight, and any open deck trailer you may require. Rome Transportation has access to standard flatbed, conestogas (or roll-tites), step-decks or curtain-side trailers as well as specialty equipment such as removable goose-neck trailers, double-drop trailers, super-b (or b-train) trailers, multi-axle roll-tite and flatbed carriers and more! We can provide any additional service required whether it is tarping, pipe stakes, dunnage, chains, or moving specialty oversized shipments.

Specialized 

At Rome, we understand that sometimes your freight requires special attention. Rome provides specialized services for all of your more complex freight. Our team of professionals is equipped to tackle your project freight, emergency freight, over-size and weight permit freight, and even your perishables but our services do not stop there. Rome is also equipped with the resources and the contacts to provide solutions with delivery – from police-escorts, crane services, tilt-n-load assistance, moffet services and more.

One of the many benefits of choosing Rome over and asset-based carrier is our ability to find an expert for everything right here within the company. No matter how special your freight is, Rome will take care of it. Pair our wide range of specialization and proven expertise, with an assemblage of equipment for any piece of freight imaginable, Rome will deliver on whatever it is you and your freight require.

Expedite 

Some freight deliveries simply cannot fail and that’s that. We have built our reputation on expediting machinery, materials, parts and products for emergency situations and providing options to give you peace-of-mind is what we do. Whether it is Sprinter Van, Box Truck, Hand Carry, Air Cargo, dedicated team service or a multi-modal combination, we have the 24/7 staff on hand to get it done and keep you updated every step of the way.
